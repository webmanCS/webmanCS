Vineyard Manor


Introducing your dream holiday home

Offering BBQ facilities, a garden as well as a terrace, Vineyard Manor at 13 Vines is located within a 10-minute drive of Somerset West. Each of the two 4-bedroom homes offer multiple living rooms, while one of them boasts a private pool. A flat-screen TV is available. Each house has a full kitchen with a dining area and bedrooms boast en suite bathrooms. A dishwasher and washing machine are also available. If you would like to discover the area, hiking is possible in the surroundings. Waterkloof Wine Estate is 2km from the property and Idiom Wines is 5 km away. Cape Town International Airport is 31 km away.

Vineyard Manor @ 13 Vines

View Reviews

13 Vines is located 45 minutes away from Cape Town City Centre, it is a 4-bedroom house on a 13-hectare grape and olive-growing estate in Somerset West. Offering stunning sunrise and sunset sea views of Gordons Bay. If you enjoy dining out, you can visit the world-renowned Waterkloof restaurant located nearby. The property allows for Muslim-friendly living with access to prayer facilities.

The expansive Vineyard Manor House proudly displays tall aesthetic ceilings and exquisite original wooden floors. It comprises 4 bedrooms and 4 bathrooms and can accommodate up to 8 guests. The main bedroom is furnished with a king-size bed. The second and third bedrooms are each furnished with a queen-size bed while the fourth bedroom is furnished with 2 single beds. Three of the bedrooms have en-suite bathrooms of which 2 are fitted with a shower, bath, toilet and basin, and the third en-suite is fitted with a shower, toilet and basin. The fourth bedroom has a dedicated bathroom fitted with a bath, shower, toilet and basin. There are also 2 guest toilets. An extra single bed/sleeper couch can be put in 2 of the bedrooms at an extra charge.

The kitchen is equipped with everything guests will need for a carefree, self-catering stay. It includes a stove, oven, fridge-freezer, microwave, dishwasher, coffee- and tea-making facilities, crockery and cutlery. The lounge has comfortable couches as well as a TV and a dining area with ample seating where guests can enjoy their meals together. Guests can also enjoy complimentary Wi-Fi. The house also features 3 beautiful fireplaces, 2 dining areas, and a library. Outside, guests have access to the expansive estate offering an outdoor entertainment area with a built-in braai, pizza oven, spacious dining table and a swimming pool, ideal for a fun day outdoors. The property has an inverter onsite that ensures the lights are kept on throughout power outages. This unforgettable getaway is perfect for newlyweds, couples, families, film crew and exhibition teams or anyone who wants to escape the busy city life.
